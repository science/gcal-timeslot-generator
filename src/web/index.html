Time Slot Generator

Generates copyable time-slot text for scheduling emails and messages. Scans your Google Calendar for free slots in the next few business days and lets you paste formatted availability into any email or chat.

Sign in with your @learningtapestry.com Google account to grant read-only access to your calendar. Your calendar data stays in your browser — no server sees it.

Access is restricted to Learning Tapestry Google Workspace accounts. Personal Gmail accounts and other company domains will be rejected at sign-in.
